Poster / Illustration

Firefly 2020 — key art that captures a night under the stars.

Role
Poster Design & Illustration
Discipline
Event / OOH
Format
Poster + transit
Tools
Illustrator · Photoshop

The brief

Design a poster for the Firefly Music Festival that captures the outdoor, camping spirit of the event — and works everywhere from a printed flyer to a large transit ad.

My approach

A moody twilight palette pairs a custom camper-van illustration with hand-built display type. Decorative elements — fireflies, music notes, foliage — bring the night-festival atmosphere to life while keeping the lineup readable.

The outcome

A flexible key-art system that holds up across scales, from a handheld flyer to a backlit out-of-home placement.
