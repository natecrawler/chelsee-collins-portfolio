Book Cover Design / Illustration

A modern cover series for three Jane Austen classics.

Role
Cover Design & Illustration
Discipline
Book Covers
Series
3 titles
Tools
Illustrator · Photoshop

The brief

Reimagine three Austen classics — Sense & Sensibility, Pride & Prejudice, and Emma — as a cohesive cover series that feels fresh and modern while honoring the source.

My approach

A custom floral pattern language unifies the set, with each title given its own color story — teal, gold, and coral. Hand-lettered titles add warmth and personality, while the shared system makes them unmistakably a series.

The outcome

Three covers that stand on their own and grow stronger together — a flexible system that could extend across the full Austen catalog.
