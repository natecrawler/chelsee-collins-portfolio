Packaging Design

Loops — minimal packaging with a single, unforgettable line.

Role
Packaging Design
Discipline
Packaging / Branding
Deliverables
Dieline + box
Tools
Illustrator

The brief

Create distinctive packaging for “Loops” that stands out on a crowded shelf with a minimal, modern aesthetic.

My approach

A matte-black box paired with a single continuous neon line motif — playful, tactile, and instantly recognizable. The looping line doubles as both brand mark and pattern, carrying seamlessly across every panel of the dieline.

The outcome

Bold, minimal packaging with strong shelf presence and a flexible visual signature.
